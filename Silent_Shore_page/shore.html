The Silent Shore

At city outskirts, beyond a ghostly neighbourhood lies the vast unknown. As you walk past the houses, you can already see the large empty field. But before you reach the plains, the pathway branches, leaving you with two options: -The left path takes you to a lonely, damp, wooden bench with an unsettling atmosphere. Dare you sit on the bench, you could embrace the vibe and feel the presence of otherworldly beings. -The right path goes along a lake. The still, reflective water looks stuck in time. Perhaps it is dominated by spirits that need to be released from a grueling time loop. Nevertheless, a serene location in which one could escape the city and ponder in peace.
