As you approach, the air grows still and heavy, amplifying the unnatural
sight ahead. Towering structures rise in disjointed, geometric patterns,
their surfaces eerily smooth yet worn, resembling colossal Lego bricks
abandoned by some long-lost civilization. The blocks are stacked
haphazardly, some jutting out precariously, creating shadows that seem
to shift and stretch unnaturally.

BrickScape Hollow

A fictional name for a structure made out of concrete blocks similar to the toy building bricks. Whether it is an unfinished project or just a place where unwanted material was discarded, the location radiates with mystery. Each one of the secluded corners contains clutters of various items. Maybe it is home for a roaming spirit, maybe there are treasures to be discovered, or maybe it is just and interesting formation. Nonetheless, a location worthy of visiting if you wish to discover its secrets.
